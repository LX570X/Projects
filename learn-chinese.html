What is Pinyin?

Pinyin is the official romanization system for Standard Mandarin Chinese. It uses Latin letters with tone marks to represent how Chinese characters are pronounced. Learning pinyin is the essential first step for any Chinese learner — it's your pronunciation roadmap. Mandarin has four tones plus a neutral tone, and the same syllable pronounced with a different tone can have a completely different meaning.
